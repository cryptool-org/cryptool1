Dialog Generate Safe Prime

This dialog is used to generate primes p of the type p = 2q+1, where again q is a prime number. The prime q is called Sophie Germain prime and p is called a safe prime. By clicking Generate prime a randomized safe prime p of the selected bitlength is chosen.

Safe primes fulfil important properties for descrete logarithm based crypto systems like the Diffie-Helmann Key-Exchange Protocol. The multiplicative group modulo p has a subgroup with a high prime number order.
