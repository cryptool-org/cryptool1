CrypTool

CrypTool is a comprehensive e-learning program which enables the use and analysis of cryptographic algorithms.
  Screenshots of CrypTool can be accessed here and at https://www.cryptool.org/content/view/23/48/lang,english/.

Further information can be found at the Starting page of the online help.

The latest version of CrypTool can be found at one of the following web sites:

    http://www.cryptool.com
