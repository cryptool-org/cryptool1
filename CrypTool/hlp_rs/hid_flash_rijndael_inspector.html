Rijndael Inspector (Menu Individual Procedures \ Visualization of Algorithms \ AES)

The Rijndael Inspector shows all intermediate results in the State matrix as the Rijndael encryption cipher progresses. The State matrix first contains one cleartext block and at the end the final ciphertext of this block.

The calculation of these intermediate results is visualized in the Rijndael Animation, developed by the same author as the Rijndael Inspector.

In the Rijndael Inspector you can arbitrarily choose the data to be processed (in the opposite to the Rijndael Animation).
