cv cryptovision

cv cryptovision is a German company specialising in the delivery of cryptographic components. With its highly qualified team of cryptographic specialists, cv cryptovision develops cryptographic hardware as well as software. The company's products and bespoke solutions are applied within e-mail programs, office applications, identity management solutions, navigation systems, motors, smart cards, measuring devices and in many other environments. Millions of people already make use of the technologies from cv cryptovision, mostly without realising it. In particular, cv cryptovision is a world wide leading business in the field of crypto=raphic methods based on elliptic curves. cv cryptovision offers unique know-how in this area.

URL for the home page on the Internet: http://www.cryptovision.com/

The company cv cryptovision delivered the crypto library "cv act library 1.3.0" used in CrypTool to implement the hybrid encryption method ECC-AES. It also helped to implement the demonstration vizualising the addition of points on elliptic curves.
