Dialogue Factorisation list (details of the factorisation)

In this dialogue displays detailed information about the last factorisation.

In the upper text field you'll find the factorised number. The list beneath shows the single steps of the factorisation:

- the factorised number,

- the two factors of the factorisation,

- the successful factorisation method and

- the factorisation time.

A double click will select a row, which appears into the lower mask with some additional information:

- the bit length of the factors and

- it will be indicated for both factors if they are prime or composite numbers (composite numbers should be factorised into additional factors).
