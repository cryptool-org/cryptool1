Dialog Details for the Current Factorization

This dialog window is shown after clicking the Details button from within the Factorization of a number dialog.

In this dialog displays detailed information about the last factorization.

In the upper text field you'll find the factorised number. The list beneath shows the single steps of the factorization:

  • the factorised number,
  • the two factors of the factorization,
  • the successful factorization method and
  • the factorization time.

A double click will select a row, which appears into the lower mask with some additional information:

  • the bit length of the factors and
  • it will be indicated for both factors if they are prime or composite numbers (composite numbers should be factorised into additional factors).
