Dialogue Introduction Diffie-Hellman Demonstration

This dialogue is supposed to introduce you to the underlying topic.

The main dialogue of the visualization of the Diffie-Hellman key exchange protocol will appear after you have clicked the OK button.

Besides a short description of the general Diffie-Hellman proceeding you can also find a check box titled Do not show this message box again, which prevents the introductory dialogue from being displayed on every restart.
