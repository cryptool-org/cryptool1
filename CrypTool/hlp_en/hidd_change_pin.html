PIN Request dialogue

In this dialogue all PINs can be entered that are necessary for importing or exporting PSEs .

For exporting a PSE to the PKCS#12 format, enter the PSE‚s PIN into the first field. You can choose a PIN for protecting the exported PKCS#12 file by entering it into the next field. Please repeat the PIN next to Verification to guard for typos.

When importing PKCS#12 files into a (newly created) PSE, enter the PKCS#12 PIN into the first field. You can choose a PIN for the new PSE by entering it into the next field. Pleas repeat the PIN next to Verification to guard for typos.

The OK button starts the export or import, while Cancel brings you back to the previous dialogue.
