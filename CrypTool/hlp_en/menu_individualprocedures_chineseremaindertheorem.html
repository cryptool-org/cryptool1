Menu Chinese Remainder Theorem Applications (Menu Individual Procedures)

The menu Chinese Remainder Theorem Applications contains the following commands:

Astronomy and Planetary Motion Interactive visualization of how long it takes planets to form a line.
Modular Forward and Backward Transformation Shows how the multiplication of two numbers can be accomplished with their modular remainders.
Secret Sharing by CRT The process of having n persons share a secret in a way that k<n persons suffice to reconstruct the secret (i.e. the concept of bank vaults).
