Dialog Generate a safe prime

This dialog is used to generate a prime p of the type p = 2q+1, where again q is a prime number.
- The prime q is called Sophie Germain prime.
- The prime p is called a safe prime.

By clicking the button Generate prime a randomized safe prime p of the selected bit length is chosen.

Safe primes fulfill important properties for descrete logarithm based crypto systems like the Diffie-Hellman key-exchange protocol.
The multiplicative group modulo p has a subgroup with a maximum prime number order.
