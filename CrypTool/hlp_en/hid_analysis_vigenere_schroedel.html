Vigenère (Schroedel) (Menu Analysis \ Symmetric Encryption (classic) \ Ciphertext-Only)

There are methods for breaking Vigenère ciphers if the key length is known. However, these methods require the ciphertext to be of a certain length and contain repetitions.

Schrodel's method overcomes these restrictions with a different approach using logic, digram frequency and language properties.

Unfortunately, Schroedel's method will fail if the key is not a dictionary word, and the analysis may be very time-consuming under certain conditions. Therefore, the quality of the underlying statistical data (digram and trigram statistics as well as a dictionary) is essential for the success of the method.
