Menu Hybrid (Menu Encrypt/Decrypt)

The menu Hybrid contains the following commands:

RSA-AES Encryption Hybrid encryption based on AES and RSA.
RSA-AES Decryption Hybrid decryption based on AES and RSA.
ECC-AES Encryption Hybrid encryption based on AES and ECC.
ECC-AES Decryption Hybrid decryption based on AES and ECC.

More information on hybrid encryption can be found in the CT Book.
