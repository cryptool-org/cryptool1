Twofish (Menu Encrypt/Decrypt \ Symmetric (modern) \ Further Algorithms)

The Twofish encryption algorithm works with a block length of 128 bits and a key length of 128, 192 or 256 bits.

CrypTool uses Twofish in CBC mode with zero initialization vector and 01-00 padding.

The key is entered in the Key entry dialog.

CrypTool contains a tool which performs a brute-force attack on the Twofish encryption algorithm.
