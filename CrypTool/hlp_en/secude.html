Secude IT Security Ltd

Secude IT Security Ltd, Darmstadt, provides modern cryptographic solutions to end users and developers.

It also offers products in the client / server, desktop and Internet areas, as well as solutions for the establishment and operation of certification structures.

URL for home page on the Internet: www.secude.com

Secude IT Security Ltd (www.secude.com) was originally involved with the implementation of the elliptic curves and has supplied the modern cryptosystems from its library (Secude library).

Secude offers applications for access control and secure e-commerce. Its core activity is the integration of cryptography into applications which can differ radically as to size and platform (from "small" Office products under Windows through to large ERP systems on virtually all platforms), including related services and training.

Secude supports all major cryptography standards, protocols and formats. Secude software is used e.g. by ABN Amro, Bank of America, Deutsche Bank, Deutsche Telekom, Procter&Gamble, SAP, Siemens, Thyssen, Volkswagen, Wells Fargo and a bunch of universities. Secude is a development partner of SAP AG, and its Secude library product runs on all platforms supported by SAP R/3 and mySAP.com and is, for example, certified for the SAP interfaces SNC (Secure Network Communications) and SSF (Secure Store and Forward). As part of the Lotus MailProtect product, Secude library protects e-mail in large Notes installations.
