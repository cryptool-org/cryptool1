Context-Sensitive Help

The Context-Sensitive Help command is used to obtain help on a particular part of CrypTool.

Two kinds of context-sensitive help are available:

Toolbar:
Keyboard: SHIFT+F1

When the context-sensitive help is activated, the mouse pointer is transformed into an arrow with a question-mark. If you click somewhere in the CrypTool window with the mouse, for example, on another button in the toolbar or a menu option, you will then be shown help on the element which you have just clicked.
