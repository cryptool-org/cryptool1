Menu Protocols (Menu Individual Procedures)

The menu Protocols contains the following commands:

Diffie-Hellman Demonstration Visualizes the Diffie-Hellman procedure.
Network Authentication Demonstrates several authentication methods and common attacks.
Secure E-Mail with S/MIME Visualizes the S/MIME protocol.

Cryptographic protocols aggregate cryptographic basis functions (called primitives) and are used between several participants. Therefore they define rules for the format, the content, the meaning and the order of exchanged messages.
