Dialog Start attack cycle

This dialog window appears after clicking the Start attack cycle button in the side-channel attack against the hybrid encryption protocol dialog.

After clicking the OK button you will be asked in which way you would like to execute the side-channel attack.

You can either

  • execute all single steps at once or
  • execute all single steps step by step.

Executing all steps at once is much quicker than executing them step by step, but you will not be able to track down relevant data changes during the attack cycle if you chose the quick method.
