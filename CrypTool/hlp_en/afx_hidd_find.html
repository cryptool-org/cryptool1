The Find dialogue box

To find a search term in the present document, it is necessary to enter the search term in the Find dialogue box and then to click on the Find Next button. If you wish to differentiate between upper and lower case letters, then you should check the Match case field. It is also possible to specify the direction of search within the document. Starting from the present cursor position, the search can proceed either upwards or downwards.

If the search term is found, it is highlighted. If the search term cannot be found in the specified direction of search, an audible signal is released.

To repeat a search, click on the Find Next button.

To replace a search term with a different text, use the menu option Replace.
