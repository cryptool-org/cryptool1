Rijndael Flow Visualization (Menu Individual Procedures \ Visualization of Algorithms \ AES)

The "AES Flow Visualisation" displays the rounds of the "AES Cipher". Every byte of the encryption is colored according to its current value. This way you can easily trace a single block within the 16 byte block while seeing that even blocks with only zeros are colored after some rounds.

Located at the top of the program are the controls. There you can generate a random 16 byte (128 bit) key and input. By clicking the "Calculation" button the visualisation is started and one round after another is displayed.


The steps visualized are:
  1. addition of the roundkey
  2. replacements with s-boxes
  3. blockshifts within rows (ShiftRow)
  4. blockmixes within columns (MixColumn)
