Dialogue RSA Cryptosystem (Menu Indiv. Procedures \ RSA demonstration)

Perhaps the most well-known asymmetric cryptosystem is the RSA algorithm, named after its authors, Ron Rivest, Adi Shamir and Leonard Adleman. In this dialogue you can carry out all three steps of the RSA cryptosystem:

  1. RSA key generation ,

  2. RSA encryption of messages RSA_demo_encryption and

  3. RSA decryption of messages RSA_demo_decryption.

For RSA encryption and RSA signature verification it suffice for input the public parameter:

  • the RSA modulus N and
  • the public RSA exponent e.

In this case the secret parameter (the Euler phi function of N and the secret key could not be known.

You can use the group box RSA using the private and public key – or using only the public key to indicate how much information is known.

The following default settings are used after each start of CrypTool:

  • In the dialogue "The RSA Cryptosystem" all fields are blank except for "2^16+1" in the field of the public key e.
  • In the dialogue "Options for RSA Encryption" the alphabet defaults the 256 characters of the 8bit ASCII code.

The values of the prime numbers, the RSA parameters and the options are retained even if the dialogue is closed and invoked again, until the CrypTool application is terminated. That way they do not have to be entered again if they shall be used a second time, but they can always be modified.

In order to transmit a message confidentially, the public key of the recipient must be known. The procedure of encoding the message with the recipients private key for privacy reasons is called encryption.

It is a special property of the RSA cryptosystem, that digital signatures can be created with same operation, if the private key of the signer is used instead of the public key of the recipient. Everyone can then verify using the public key, that the signature was created by the holder of the corresponding private key.

The story, The Dialogue of sisters [c‚t magazine, issue 25/99], describes a variant of the RSA cryptosystem that is suitable for hand calculation. This variant can be selected under Options for the alphabet and the number system after a small RSA key has been generated.

The example below illustrates the RSA encryption of the following message:

"Verkaufen Sie am 14.07.00 meine Aktienpakete, und kaufen Sie mir dafür eine Südsee Insel"

[Sell my shares on 14 July 2000 and use the proceeds to buy me a South Sea island].

This operation entails the following steps:

First of all the two prime numbers p and q have to be chosen (the simplest way to achieve this is to click on Generate prime numbers), then the public key e has to be entered. After that the secret key d can be calculated by clicking on "Update parameters".

In the Options for the alphabet and the number system dialogue you can make further adjustments to the settings for encryption prior to carrying out the encryption or decryption, namely:

  • Alphabet options
  • The "Dialogue of sisters" RSA variant
  • The number system in which the numbers for RSA encryption and decryption are to be presented

To initiate encryption, entered the plaintext and then click on the Encrypt button.
