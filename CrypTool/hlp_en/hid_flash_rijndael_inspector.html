Rijndael Inspector (Menü Einzelverfahren \ Visualisierung von Algorithmen \ AES)

The Rijndael Inspector shows intermediate results after each step of each round of the Rijndael encryption algorithm.

The calculation of these intermediate results is visualized in the Rijndael Animation, developed by the same author as the Rijndael Inspector.

Here you can arbitrarily choose the data to be processed (in the opposite to the Rijndael Animation).
