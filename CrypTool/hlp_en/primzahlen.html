Prime Numbers

Detailed information on prime numbers can be found in the script.

CrypTool offers many functions regarding prime numbers:

  • Prime numbers can be created in a specific range of values (using the dialog Prime Number Generation).
  • Numbers can be tested whether they are prime (using the dialog Prime Number Test).
  • Compound numbers can be decomposed into their prime factors (using the dialog Factorization of a Number).
  • Further functions can be found in the menu RSA Cryptosystem.

    • Apart from that you can interactively learn about prime number theory using the learning tool for number theory (menu Number Theory - Interactive).

    Prime numbers are one of the most interesting mathematic fields. They are an integral part of everyday life, but they still yield a lot of secrets: see http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prime_number.
