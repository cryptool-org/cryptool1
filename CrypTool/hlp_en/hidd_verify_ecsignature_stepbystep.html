Dialog Signature verification - step by step

This dialog is opened during the verification of an elliptic curve based signature, if the Display intermediate results option was selected in the Signature verification dialog.

The Signed message field shows the whole message, i.e. signature plus message.

The actual signature is displayed in the Signature field. The Octal, Decimal, Hexadecimal and Octets options allow you to choose how the signature should be presented.

The individual steps are shown in the Single step signature-verification field.

To view the next step involved in signature verification, click on the Continue > button.
