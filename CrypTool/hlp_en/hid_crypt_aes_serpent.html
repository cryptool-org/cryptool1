Serpent (AES candidate) (Menu Encrypt/Decrypt \ Symmetric (modern) \ Further Algorithms)

The Serpent encryption algorithm works with a block length of 128 bits and a key length of 256 bits. Any keys shorter than this are padded out to 256 bits.

CrypTool uses Serpent in CBC mode with zero initialization vector and 01-00 padding.

The key is entered in the Key entry dialog.

CrypTool contains a tool which performs a brute-force attack on the Serpent encryption algorithm.
