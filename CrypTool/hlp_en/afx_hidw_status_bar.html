Status bar

The status bar is displayed on the lower edge of the CrypTool window. You can choose whether to display or conceal the status bar by selecting the Status Bar menu option from the menu View.

As you move through the menu using the CURSOR KEYS, the left-hand area of the status bar indicates the function of the menu entries. Similarly, descriptions on the effect of the various buttons in the icon bar are also displayed in this area.

The right-hand area of the status bar indicates which of the following keys have been specified.

Display Description
CAP The CAPS LOCK key has been activated.
NUM The NUM LOCK key has been activated.
SCRL The SCROLL LOCK key has been activated.
