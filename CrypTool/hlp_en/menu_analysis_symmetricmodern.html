Menu Symmetric Encryption (modern) (Menu Analysis)

The menu Symmetric Encryption (modern) contains the following commands for the brute-force analysis of the following encryption algorithms:

IDEA Automatic analysis of IDEA.
RC2 Automatic analysis of RC2.
RC4 Automatic analysis of RC4.
DES (ECB) Automatic analysis of DES (ECB).
DES (CBC) Automatic analysis of DES (CBC).
Triple DES (ECB) Automatic analysis of Triple DES (ECB).
Triple DES (CBC) Automatic analysis of Triple DES (CBC).
Rijndael (AES) Automatic analysis of Rijndael.
Further Algorithms Automatic analysis of other algorithms.
