Elliptic Curves

CrypTool can use elliptic curves for digital signatures (Menu Digital Signatures/PKI). Further information can be found in the CT Book.

How to sign documents based on elliptic curve keys

The menu item Key Management \ Key Generation opens the following dialog:

Please fill in the mandatory fields "Last Name", "First Name", "PIN " and "PIN verification". Don‚t forget to check "Algorithm: Elliptic curves" and press "Generate new key pair"

Now you can use the menu items Digital Signatures \ Sign Message and Digital Signatures \ Verify Signature to digitally sign documents reps. verify their authenticity. Remark: You can use the scenario RSA crypto system (signatur) with elliptic curve keys, too.
