Factorisation attack on RSA

The RSA system‚s security is based on the fact that it is practically impossible to compute from the public RSA modulus N its two prime factors p and q, if N is sufficiently large.

For small key lengths you can try to factor N into its two prime factors p and q.

After you have entered the public modulus N and the public key e into the RSA Cryptosystem dialog, you can start this factorisation by pressing the button Factorise RSA modulus

After successful factorisation the private parameters are entered automatically into the dialog and you can now decrypt and sign data.
