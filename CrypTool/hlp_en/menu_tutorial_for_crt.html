The Applications of the Chinese Remainder Theorem (CRT) submenu (Menu Individual Procedures)

The Applications of the Chinese Remainder Theorem (CRT) submenu, which is part of the Individual Procedures menu, contains the following options:

Astronomy and planetary Motion  Interactive visualization which shows how long it takes until planets line up in a row.
Modular transformation

Shows, how the multiplication of two numbers can be accomplished also with their modular number remainders.

Secret Sharing by CRT

Procedure for distributing a secret to n people, with the option that it can be rebuilt by k < n people. (e.g. as a security concept for the big bank vaults).
