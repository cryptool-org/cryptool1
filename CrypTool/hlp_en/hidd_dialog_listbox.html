Dialog Certificates in the CA Database

For RSA and DSA key pairs CrypTool issues certificates.

A list of all the available RSA and DSA keys is displayed in the dialog (these are the only ones for which CrypTool holds certificates). From this list one can choose which certificate should be displayed. To view a certificate, select the required entry from the list and click on Show certificate. The information shown on the certificate includes the period of validity of the certificate, the originator and a fingerprint of the public key.
